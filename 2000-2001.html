Fulfilling on the Vision Statement in more ways than one, in its seventh year in existence this "21st university for the 21st Century" acquires a public radio station, rewards award-winning students, and celebrates its nationally recognized faculty. (CSUMB is given this appellation for being the 21st campus in the California State University system, and having opened just prior to the turn of the new century.)
