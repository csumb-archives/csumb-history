The Newman Club, a staple on college campuses throughout the world, sponsors a Catholic Mass several Sundays in December. Club members work with clergy from the local community to arrange for regular services on campus beginning in the spring semester.

Following the ideals of John Newman&#8212the Cardinal-Deacon philosopher, man of letters, and the most illustrious of English converts to the Catholic Church&#8212the Newman Club's main goal is "to be a spiritual support to the students during their educational journey while residing on university campuses." Activities are organized to lend educational, social, and spiritual support to all campus community members.
