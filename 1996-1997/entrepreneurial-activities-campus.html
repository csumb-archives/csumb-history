In addition to CSUMB's unique academic activities and student affairs, there are a wide variety of entrepreneurial activities going on around campus. One such project, called Intern-Net, is in its final stages of development. Led by Leslie Davis and Jeff Munks, Intern-Net is a revenue-generating venture that creates a web-based subscription service that matches qualified interns with regional organizations looking for interns.

There are also 23 official student clubs-all initiated and led by students-with the support of one staff and one faculty advisor. Among the standout clubs is the Multicultural Club, which sponsors two dances and activities around Día de los Muertos. Planet Otter, CSUMB's environmental club, is already planning the Second Annual Earth Day on April 22, after sponsoring the highly successful First Annual Earth Day.
