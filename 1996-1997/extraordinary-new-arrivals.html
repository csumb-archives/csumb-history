--- layout: history title: Extraordinary New Arrivals ---

By the time classes start again for the spring semester on January 29, 190 new students and eight faculty members have arrived on campus. Among the faculty members is
Dr. Amalia Mesa-Bains, who has been active on campus for the previous year as the Interim Director of Visual and Public Arts (VPA). Now she takes the "Interim" off her title and begins officially to lead the already highly regarded VPA program. An internationally recognized artist, Mesa-Bains has had her work featured in major exhibits at the Whitney Museum of American Art, the Museum of American Art at the Smithsonian, the Los Angeles Museum of Contemporary Art, and in myriad venues internationally.

The most recent CSUMB faculty additions are: Nubra Floyd (Human Development Professor); Laraine Lomax (Professor of Ecological Economics [ESSP]); Scot Rafkin (Atmospheric Scientist); John Creech (Electronic Reference Librarian); John Ober (Librarian for Electronic Resources); Mary Ito Dennison (Electronic Reference Services Librarian); and Jo Ann Cannon (Wellness, Physical Education & Recreation).
