--- layout: history title: Day of the Dead Día de los Muertos ---

With the combined help of Center One institutes, Visual & Public Art (VPA) organizes a gallery exhibit, a temporary mural, an outdoor altar, and a performance celebrating the Mexican tradition of honoring and remembering the dead. Native dancers ("danzantes"), poets from across the faculty and student body, musicians, representatives of native communities, historic military participants, and students memorialize ancestors through remembrances in song and word.

The gallery exhibition includes work by students Gloria Leno, Kathleen Biersteaker, Brock Essick, Erik Chappins, Beth Quiros, and Human Communication (HCOM) faculty. The outdoor candlelight ceremony procession uses projected slide images designed by one of the VPA's 3-D classes.
