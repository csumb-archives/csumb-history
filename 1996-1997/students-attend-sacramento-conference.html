--- layout: history title: Students Attend Sacramento Conference ---

From April 25-28, students represent CSUMB in Sacramento at the second annual California Higher Education Student Summit (CHESS), organized by the California State Student Association (CSSA). Kendia Herrington, Ryan Rutenschroer, Zach Turner, Chris Holguin, Sarah Emerson, Chris Horton, Sara McClellan, Josh Callahan, and Heidi Zuercher attend the summit.

The CSSA provides a forum for the more than 330,000 voices of the nation's largest higher education system to unite and address the critical issues and concerns of all student bodies. Students from all over the state (including community college students) come together to share ideas, participate in informational workshops, and actively advocate and lobby for student rights.
