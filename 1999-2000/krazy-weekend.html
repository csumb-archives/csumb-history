--- layout: history title: A "Krazy" Weekend ---

November 20 is Kelp Kraze Day, which answers a question posed by many members of nearby communities: "What's going on out there at CSUMB?"

This first annual event combines music, food, live entertainment, a library book sale, a film festival, varied "edutainment" activities, tours of the campus, and fun for the entire family. Bringing members of the campus and nearby communities together on Sixth Avenue (literally on the street!) amid the university's multiple state-of-the-art facilities, Kelp Kraze Day caps an eventful weekend.

A tailgate party held at the Black Box Cabaret before CSUMB's Homecoming Women's Basketball game on Friday night serves as the tip-off. Then on Saturday morning, runners participated in a 4.8-mile Turkey Trot. At the same time, the annual On-Campus Admissions Day, hosted in the University Center by the Student Outreach and Recruitment staff, offers over 700 prospective students the opportunity to attend workshops, tour the campus, and be admitted on the spot by submitting a completed application meeting minimum requirements. What's going on? Perhaps the busiest weekend in CSUMB history to date!
