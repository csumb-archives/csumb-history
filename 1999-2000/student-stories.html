Amy Lauder

Then: Amy Lauder is a 2000 graduate from CSUMB in Liberal Studies, with a Recreation emphasis. Originally from Orange County, Amy came north in January 1997-an upper-division transfer from a community college in southern California. She says she came to CSUMB "because it didn't have athletic scholarships [in 1997] and didn't have a Greek system; and because they did have big housing, so I didn't have to live in the dorms; I could live in an apartment."
