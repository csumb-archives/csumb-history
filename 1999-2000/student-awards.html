--- layout: history title: Student Awards ---

CSUMB announces the winners of four Outstanding Senior Awards and the award recipients are recognized on May 19 at the Commencement Awards Ceremony in the University Center.

President's Award for Student Exemplary Achievement ~ José Salvador Aguilera

The award formally recognizes one outstanding graduating senior who has demonstrated exceptional contributions to CSUMB and the greater communities. This year's award winner is Human Communication major José Aguilera. As the recipient of this award, Aguilera gives the student's speech at Commencement. Aguilera, from East Salinas, has attended CSUMB since the fall of 1996.

Tri-County Achievement Award ~ Ernesto Sanchez

The Tri-County Award winner is Human Communication major Ernesto Sanchez, who coordinated a special project in service to the Tri-County community. Sanchez, from nearby Pajaro, has participated actively as a positive agent of change and is a role model to other Tri-County students.

Social Justice Award ~ Tyana Thayer

The Social Justice Award winner is Earth Systems Science and Policy major Tyana Thayer. Thayer is formally recognized because she has exemplified the social justice tenets of CSUMB's Vision Statement. Thayer was raised in Mammoth Lakes, California, and was the first person ever admitted to CSUMB.

Alumni Association Vision Award ~ Arnold J. Valdez

The Alumni Association Vision Award formally recognizes one outstanding graduating senior who is the first person in his or her family to earn a college degree and who has embodied the values and goals expressed in CSUMB's vision statement. This award goes to Social and Behavioral Sciences major Arnold J. Valdez (from Corpus Christi, Texas), who has served as a role model for his family and community.
