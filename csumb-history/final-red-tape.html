--- layout: history title: The Final Red Tape ---

Executive Dean Hank Hendrickson, in his chronology of events recorded at the time, makes this terse entry in June 1994:

"$15 million in federal funding provided through EDA [Economic Development Administration]. Notwithstanding the fact that Congress has appropriated $15 million for the project and the money has been transferred from the DOD [Department of Defense] to the EDA, we must now submit a grant application to get the money encumbered by EDA for this project before the money can be used by the campus. (What a fun time we are having)."
