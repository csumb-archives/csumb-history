--- layout: history title: Sailing for Mariners of All Ages ---

Thanks to the CSUMB boating program, a fleet of 15 vessels is available to community members&#8212through a variety of programs&#8212making it easy to sail the incomparable Monterey Bay Marine Sanctuary. The year-round sailing curriculum at CSUMB provides varied boat handling instruction, so almost anyone can learn basic sailing aboard one of the nine 13-15 foot Flying Juniors or earn American Sailing Association (ASA) certification aboard four 19-29 foot sloops.

The pride of the fleet is the 56' Rose Evelyn, a great vessel for offshore blue water sailing. Whale n' Sail voyages aboard the Rose Evelyn occur each winter as the gray whales migrate south past the Monterey Bay. With a total of six passengers on board, these three-hour natural history sails provide an intimate, quiet viewing of these majestic creatures. The CSUMB Youth Extreme Sailing (YES!) program for kids aged eight to eighteen is held throughout the summer months. Sailing aboard the Flying Juniors, youngsters learn about boating safety, rigging, sea and wind conditions, teamwork, nautical terminology, knot tying, racing, and much more.
