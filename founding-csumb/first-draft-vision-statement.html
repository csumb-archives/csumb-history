After many drafts, the Monterey Bay Faculty Advisory Committee (MBFAC)-organized by Provost Steven Arvizu and made up of faculty from the California State University system-approves a draft Vision Statement for the new California State University, Monterey Bay. This Vision Statement will be refined through numerous drafts over the course of the year.
