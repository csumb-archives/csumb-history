--- layout: history title: 1992 ---

In 1992, the idea of a new campus of California State University on Fort Ord is developed, and planning and negotiations begin. A core group of individuals with remarkable determination press forward into uncertainty, persuading lawmakers and officials one by one-from the capitol in Sacramento to the basement of the Pentagon-to obtain the approvals necessary to make the dream of a major university in the Monterey Bay area a reality.
