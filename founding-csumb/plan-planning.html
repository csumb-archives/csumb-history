--- layout: history title: A "Plan for Planning" ---

California State University (CSU) presents its initial plan and vision for CSU Monterey Bay to the Fort Ord planning team on June 9. It is titled "Fort Ord-Plan for Planning and Vision Statement." The vision of the new university was based on the following goals:

  • To be a prototypical university for the next century
  • To advance innovative approaches to traditional educational problems
  • To attract a prestigious faculty
  • To provide a model for the way education must adjust to meet the challenges of a new century
  • To develop academic programs that transcend traditional walls and shape the institution
  • To create an institution of distinction in its commitment to teaching, research and public service

Three Fort Ord planning committee task forces (on educational mission, institutional advancement, and educational environment) will add substance to these goals in the months ahead.

Three weeks later, Chancellor Munitz commits $3 million in 1993-1994 for new initiatives relating to Fort Ord.
