--- layout: history title: A Major University at Fort Ord? ---

An article in the Monterey County Herald on July 28 trumpets the possibility of an independent university at Fort Ord before the idea of a stand-alone campus has been publicly discussed by community leaders. "No final decision is near, but it was reassuring to learn last week that a major university [emphasis added] is one of the possibilities for the sprawling military facility."

The article vaults the issue of a stand-alone campus at Fort Ord into wide public discussion.
