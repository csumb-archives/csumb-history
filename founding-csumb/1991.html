--- layout: history title: 1991 ---

As Fort Ord is indeed marked for closure in 1991, the community entertains the idea of a university presence at Fort Ord. Through conversations between a number of community, education, and government representatives, the concept grows from an off-campus center of San Jose State University to a major, stand-alone university at Fort Ord. This is a year of exploration of the idea.
