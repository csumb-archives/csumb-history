--- layout: history title: The Team Is Now Eight ---

By November 1993, the core team has grown to eight people. Added to the initial team (Hendrickson, Pantoja, Rey and Arvizu) are John Miller, Engineer; Kelsey Escoto, Secretary to the Provost; David Salazar, Planner; and Carl Berman, technician to the Provost.
