CSUMB accepts an invitation to join the California Collegiate Athletic Association (CCAA), the NCAA's Division II intercollegiate athletics conference.

"CSU Monterey Bay will be a good addition to the California Collegiate Athletic Association," says CCAA Commissioner Robert Hiegert. "Our members were impressed with CSUMB's strong interest in joining the CCAA, and the outstanding potential the campus and athletic program offer."

The Otters-who since the university's opening have competed in the California Pacific Conference of the National Association of Intercollegiate Athletics (NAIA)-will begin competing in the CCAA in 2004-2005, the first year of a four-year NCAA provisional period.
