--- layout: history title: OnCampus Admission Day ---

CSUMB hosts an On-Campus Admission Day on November 22. CSU-eligible students from all over California attend. The benefit of on-campus admission is that students know if they are accepted to the university at the end of the day, rather than waiting the standard eight weeks to get the results of an application.

"This was a great way to apply to college," says a student about the event. "It took the stress out of the application process and I got all my questions answered about CSUMB in one day."

The day's agenda includes tours of the campus as well as workshops on applications, academic majors, financial aid, and the Education Opportunity Program (EOP). The event gives everyone the chance to learn about CSUMB's outcomes-based curriculum and personally-tailored majors. Approximately 150 students and their parents attend, and more than half are admitted to CSUMB on the spot. The previous year's event attracted 90 students seeking on-site admission; of those applicants, 55 were accepted.
