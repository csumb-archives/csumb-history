--- layout: history title: Generosity Abounds ---

CSUMB kicks off its first Annual Fund campaign in June. The purpose of the campaign is to increase the scholarship and financial assistance available to CSUMB students. Because CSUMB is a state-assisted (not state-supported) institution, generous contributions are vital to the university's success.

First National Bank of Central California becomes the first major contributor to the campaign with a gift of $40,000 in matching funds that establishes the first endowed scholarship fund at CSUMB. As others contribute to the fund, First National Bank matches their donations up to the $40,000 limit.

The Payroll Deduction Program is an important initiative that adds to the Annual Fund and accesses the First National Bank matching funds. Following the lead of Octavio Villalpando, CSUMB Director of Planning and Assessment, many staff and faculty members-who already contribute greatly through daily efforts on behalf of CSUMB-commit part of their pay back to the university through this program. Other staff and faculty members choose to make yearly donations to the Annual Fund, including CSUMB's entire President's Cabinet.
