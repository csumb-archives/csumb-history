--- layout: history title: Planning Faculty Members ---

The 13 planning faculty members-like the many CSUMB denizens who have carried the torch thus far-are called upon to do far more than what their original training and experiences have prepared them for. The new university's diverse faculty consists of 13 individuals, each reflecting the cross-cultural, multidisciplinary backgrounds they expect in their future students. Almost all of them have either been students or faculty members at a CSU campus. They are:

  • Judith Francisca Baca, visual arts
  • Manuel Luis Carlos, anthropology
  • Michael E. Connor, psychology
  • William D. Head, oceanography, marine sciences
  • Victoria Wai Jew, bilingual education, curriculum, instruction
  • Suzanne Lacy, fine arts, visual arts
  • Josina M. Makau, communications, rhetoric, critical thinking
  • Marsha Moroh, computer science
  • J. Ken Nishita, biology, health, biopsychology
  • Maria de la Luz Reyes, teacher education, Chicano studies, educational psychology
  • Robert P. van Spyk, geography, business computers, telecommunications
  • Luis Valdez, teledramatic arts, film, theater
  • Christine Sleeter, teacher education, curriculum, instruction (started in May 1995)

NOTE: As of the end of the 2003-2004 academic year, only four of the original 13-Marsha Moroh, Josina Makau, Bill Head, and Ken Nishita-continued to work full-time at CSUMB. Three-Manuel Carlos, Bob van Spyk, and Christine Sleeter-had retired or were in the process. Luis Valdez was on leave.
