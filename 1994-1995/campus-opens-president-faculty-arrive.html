--- layout: history title: Campus Opens As President & Faculty Arrive ---

In January, the campus officially opens with the arrival of 17 planning faculty members and administrators (with Dorothy Lloyd as Founding Administrator). All university offices relocate to campus. There are now 57 employees at CSUMB. And now the real work begins: remodeling; planning; hiring new faculty; developing curriculum and infrastructure; and establishing a collective/community identity. A process that would normally take from three to seven years to execute properly will have to be completed in eight months. Facilities have to be renovated, equipment purchased, policies and procedures developed, roles and responsibilities defined and clarified, curriculum planned and implemented, students admitted, and additional faculty and staff members hired.
