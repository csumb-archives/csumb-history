--- layout: history title: October Campus Events ---

Campus Events in October include:

  • Visiting Artist Gustavo Leclerc, "Defining Public Space through Cultural History," at the Musical Hall Auditorium (October 25)
  • Monterey Bay Film Fest 2000. Sponsored by the United Nations, Coast Weekly, and CSUMB's Institute for Teledramatic Arts and Technology, and held at the World Theater. The first extension film festival of the United Nations Film Festival held in Palo Alto, the festival features films from twelve different countries (October 27 & 28)
  • The Brain Journey 2000:"Technology, Medicine, and Education for Attention Deficit," at University Center (October 28)
  • World-Class Tap Dancer Savion Glover, "The Art of Improvisation with Dance and Music," at the World Theater (October 31)
