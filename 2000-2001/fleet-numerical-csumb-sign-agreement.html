--- layout: history title: Fleet Numerical & CSUMB Sign Agreement ---

CSUMB and the Fleet Numerical Meteorology and Oceanography Center, a division of the Navy, sign a memorandum of understanding that allows for the development of joint programs between the two organizations.

According to the agreement signed by CSUMB President Peter Smith and Fleet Numerical Commanding Officer Captain J.W. Swaykos, the groups will develop six joint programs and projects, which incorporate the following:

  • Internships ~ Fleet Numerical will create internship positions for qualified students, and CSUMB faculty will be invited to recommend students for those internship positions.
  • Work-Study ~ CSUMB will recommend qualified students to Fleet Numerical for work-study opportunities.
  • Degree Completion ~ Employees of Fleet Numerical will be encouraged to complete undergraduate degrees at CSUMB. CSUMB faculty will serve as advisors for those students.
  • Service Learning ~ CSUMB students will be encouraged to participate in Service Learning experiences as defined by Fleet Numerical staff.
  • Part-Time Faculty ~ Qualified Fleet Numerical staff may serve as part-time faculty in various disciplines at CSUMB.
  • Curriculum Development ~ CSUMB and Fleet Numerical will investigate the possibility of joint curriculum development, building from their shared strengths.
