In its third year accepting students, CSUMB solidifies and expands its community in many ways: the Panetta Institute's Leon Panetta Lecture Series kicks off its first year with extraordinary guests and moderators; former congressmen spend a week on campus rubbing elbows with students; the entire campus community bands together aggressively to fight apparent racism within its own ranks; the CSUMB Master Plan is approved by CSU's Board of Trustees; and a notable CSUMB graduate declines a graduate school opportunity at Harvard for an even better offer elsewhere.
