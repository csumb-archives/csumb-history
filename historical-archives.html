--- layout: history title: Historical Archives ---

A number of archives provide historical perspective on CSUMB's first ten years. Hard copy archives of many materials can be accessed in the CSUMB Library.

The State of the University Addresses, delivered to the campus community each January, provide annual insight into campus progress.

Archived copies of the student-run newspaper, The Otter Realm, provide a student perspective.
