--- layout: history title: 19961997 ---

CSUMB's second year accepting students (and third year overall) is seen as the year for transforming the campus from a fledgling early-stage endeavor into a stable, reputable, long-term enterprise. Despite some unexpected roadblocks (e.g., lingering confusion over ULRs), the ever-growing campus continues to lure quality people to contribute to the school's development.
