--- layout: history title: CSUMB Ranks High in Survey ---

In January, CSUMB receives a report card from the National Survey of Student Engagement: The College Student Report (NSSE). The survey is unique in that it provides a measure of what America's university students believe about the quality of their higher education experience, rather than relying strictly on statistical measures.

The survey compares 470 colleges and universities around the country. Results are based on comments from 155,000 freshmen and seniors in five key areas. CSUMB is ranked in the top one percent by seniors surveyed&#8212and in the top 10 percent by first-year students&#8212in "Active and Collaborative Learning." The university ranks in the top 10 percent&#8212with both seniors and first- year students&#8212in "Enriching Educational Experiences." CSUMB is rated in the top 20 percent of participating universities, by seniors and first-year students,in "Student Interaction with Faculty."

CSUMB is ranked in the top 20 percent with seniors, and in the top half with first-year students, in "Level of Academic Challenge. " The university is rated in the top 40 percent, by both seniors and first-year students, in the Supportive Campus Environment category.
