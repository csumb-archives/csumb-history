--- layout: history title: Sixth Commencement Ceremony ---

CSUMB holds its sixth commencement ceremony May 25 at Freeman Stadium, adjacent to the campus. Over 450 degrees and 100 teaching credentials are conferred.

An estimated 5,000 enthusiastic visitors from far and wide converge on the stadium to acknowledge the academic success of hundreds of CSUMB students. After receiving an honorary degree recognizing his numerous achievements in defense of civil liberties and social justice, Bishop Samuel Ruiz Garcia presents the keynote address.

Brenda Guadalupe Valles is the student speaker. Valles, the first in her family to attend college, is honored for exemplifying the CSUMB Vision. She is named winner of the President's Award for Exemplary Student Achievement.

"Even if the whole world tells you it's not possible, you can do it," Valles tells the audience. "Don't be afraid of taking risks. Do it for yourselves."
