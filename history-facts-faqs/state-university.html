--- layout: history title: State of the University ---

Each year, the CSUMB President presents a State of the University Address. From 1996 through 2005, the address came at the start of the calendar year. The address now comes near the start of the academic year.

Dianne F. Harrison, Ph.D., CSUMB president, delivered the 2010 address as part of the Day of Welcome Ceremony on August 17.

These pages provide an archive of the addresses in various formats. Please choose a year from the leftside navigation.
